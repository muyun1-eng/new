Her Work

Han So-hee (Korean: 한소희; born Lee So-hee (이소희); November 18, 1994) is a South Korean actress and model. She began her career as a supporting character in the television series Money Flower (2017), 100 Days My Prince (2018),and Abyss (2019) before transitioning into lead roles in the JTBC series The World of the Married (2020) and Nevertheless (2021), and the Netflix-produced My Name (2021).
